Festive Episodes

16th February 6:30 PM Onwards
PANTHAKAAL "Embarking on Love's Journey - A Prelude to Forever"

Elevate the anticipation of union with the symbolic Pandakkal Pooja, a day-before ritual infused with familial reverence. A bamboo pole, embodying fertility, is erected to ward off evil glances and seek blessings for a harmonious marriage journey. Gathered under familial gaze, prayers ensue, and the pole adorned with Manjal and Kumkum stands tall, ready to usher in the divine blessings.
55, Jalan 7/3a, Seksyen 7, Bandar Baru Bangi, 43650 Selangor, Malaysia

17th February 6:30 PM Onwards
NALUNGU "Colors of Joy, Bonds of Love - A Playful Celebration"

As evening hues paint the sky, the bride and groom partake in the unique Nalangu ritual. Seated on mats in the marriage pandal, a playful exchange unfolds. Turmeric paste, symbolizing blessings and vibrancy, is shared as they stretch out legs, embodying a journey marked by mutual care. Laughter echoes in this intimate moment, creating a canvas of joy for the love story unfolding.
55, Jalan 7/3a, Seksyen 7, Bandar Baru Bangi, 43650 Selangor, Malaysia

02nd March 6:30 PM Onwards
RECEPTION "Dancing into Forever - A Night of Love and Laughter"

Step into the grandeur of the wedding reception, a jubilant affair held after the sacred ceremony. The newlyweds receive society, friends, and family, marking their debut as a married couple. Amidst enchanting decor and joyful toasts, the reception becomes a celebration of love, weaving the first threads of their shared journey into the tapestry of forever.
Sri Annai Mahal A/C, Thiruvottiyur High Rd, Old Washermanpet, Chennai

17th February 8:30 AM Onwards
MEHANDHI "In Henna and Happiness - Love Unveils Its Beauty"

Immerse in the Mehndi Ceremony, a tapestry of positive spirits and well-wishes. The night before the wedding, henna is artfully applied, symbolizing good health and prosperity for the bride's journey. A celebration where female bonds intertwine, Mehndi's cooling touch serves as a prelude to joy, relieving any pre-wedding stress. The intricate patterns unfold, echoing the beauty of an upcoming union.
55, Jalan 7/3a, Seksyen 7, Bandar Baru Bangi, 43650 Selangor, Malaysia

18th February 8:30 AM Onwards
WEDDING CEREMONY "Sacred Vows, Eternal Promises - Our Journey Begins"

Feel the pulse of excitement as the wedding ceremony commences. Amidst the rhythmic beats of traditional Mangala Vadhyam, the groom ties the sacred Mangal Sutra around the bride's neck, symbolizing eternal union. Surrounded by the gathered crowd, the air is thick with joy, and the sacred vows resonate, echoing the beginning of a timeless journey together.
Sri Renganathar Temple, 4300, Jalan Ikram-Uniten, Kawasan Institusi Bangi, 43000 Kajang, Selangor, Malaysia
